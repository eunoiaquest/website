We're excited to launch the Eunoia Quest blog! This is where we'll be sharing our journey building a gamified mental health screening platform, as well as insights from the intersection of gaming, machine learning, and psychology.

## What to Expect

Here's what you can look forward to reading about:

- **Product Updates** — New features, improvements, and milestones as we grow the platform.
- **Science & Research** — The clinical research and machine learning techniques that power our assessments.
- **Industry Insights** — Trends in digital mental health, gamification, and healthcare technology.
- **Team Stories** — Behind-the-scenes looks at the people building Eunoia Quest.

## Why a Blog?

Transparency and education are core to our mission. Mental health screening shouldn't be a black box. By sharing how our technology works and the evidence behind it, we hope to build trust with both clinicians and the people they serve.

## Stay Tuned

We have a lot of exciting content planned. If you'd like to stay updated, feel free to [get in touch](/#contact) — we'd love to hear from you.
